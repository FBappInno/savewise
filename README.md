# SaveWise

> Your personal AI knowledge companion.

SaveWise helps you remember what matters.

Instead of collecting endless bookmarks, saved posts and videos across different platforms, SaveWise builds a private, AI-powered knowledge base that helps you rediscover information when you actually need it.

---

## ✨ Vision

The internet is full of valuable information.

The problem isn't finding it.

The problem is remembering it.

SaveWise helps people capture, organize and reconnect their digital knowledge — while keeping them in control of their own data.

---

## 🚀 Planned Features

- Save content from multiple platforms
- AI-generated summaries
- Smart tagging and categorization
- Semantic search
- Personal knowledge spaces
- Cross-platform synchronization
- Privacy-first architecture
- Proactive reminders and rediscovery
